# Project Assumptions

## Product
- **Product**: Traditional Chinese Hanfu clothing collection
- **Target Market**: US/EU English-speaking consumers interested in cultural fashion, cosplay, and unique clothing
- **Category**: Traditional Chinese Clothing / Cultural Fashion
- **Price Point**: $89-$129 (premium but accessible)

## Audience
- Cultural enthusiasts interested in Chinese heritage
- Cosplay and costume enthusiasts
- Fashion-forward individuals looking for unique pieces
- People attending cultural festivals, weddings, or themed events

## Emotional Triggers
- Desire to embrace cultural heritage and timeless elegance
- Appreciation for handcrafted, artisanal quality
- Wanting to stand out with unique, meaningful fashion
- Connection to Chinese culture and aesthetics

## Technical Decisions
- **Framework**: Next.js 16 with App Router
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Rendering**: SSR/SSG for SEO optimization
- **State Management**: None needed for MVP (static content)
- **Payment**: Placeholder checkout URL (to be replaced with Stripe/payment provider)

## Content Strategy
- Emotional storytelling approach
- Focus on cultural heritage and craftsmanship
- Emphasis on quality and authenticity
- Trust-building through social proof and guarantees

## SEO Strategy
- Target keywords: hanfu, traditional chinese clothing, chinese fashion, cultural wear
- Long-tail keywords: buy hanfu online, hanfu for cosplay, traditional chinese dress
- Local SEO: Not applicable for initial launch

## Shipping & Fulfillment
- Worldwide shipping available
- Standard shipping: 7-14 business days
- Express options available
- 30-day return policy

## Compliance Notes
- No medical or health claims
- No copyrighted brand names or protected characters
- Authentic cultural designs (not replicas of specific historical artifacts)
- Standard clothing safety requirements apply

## Media Assets Needed
- Hero product image/video
- Individual product photos
- Lifestyle/context photos
- Size guide images
- Brand logo and assets

## Testing Priorities
1. Mobile responsiveness
2. Page load speed
3. Checkout flow
4. SEO metadata
5. Cross-browser compatibility
