# Category Positioning Guide

## Pet products

Best emotional triggers:

- boredom relief
- owner guilt
- playful bonding
- daily enrichment
- less loneliness

Avoid unsupported safety or veterinary claims.

Required trust details:

- material
- size
- cleaning method
- supervision guidance
- battery/charging notes if electronic

## Toys and gifts

Best emotional triggers:

- surprise
- collecting
- nostalgia
- giftability
- desk display

Watch for:

- age labeling
- choking hazards
- copyrighted characters
- battery compliance

## Desk gadgets

Best emotional triggers:

- productivity mood
- workspace identity
- tactile satisfaction
- “small upgrade” feeling

Trust details:

- dimensions
- compatibility
- power requirements
- material
- warranty

## Smart hardware / robotics

Best emotional triggers:

- futuristic interaction
- companionship
- creative expression
- curiosity

Trust details:

- what it can and cannot do
- app/Bluetooth/Wi-Fi requirements
- battery life
- privacy
- warranty
- certifications placeholder

## Beauty / wellness

Use extra caution.

Do not make medical claims. Use “routine”, “comfort”, “appearance”, or “self-care” framing unless claims are legally verified.

## Apparel

Not ideal for beginner cross-border validation due to high return risk.

If used, include:

- size guide
- material
- model sizing
- return policy
- shipping policy
